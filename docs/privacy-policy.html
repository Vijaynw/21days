1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Account Information: Email address and profile name when you create an account (optional)
  • Habit Data: The habits you create, track, and your completion history
  • App Preferences: Your settings and customization choices

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance (can be disabled)
  • Crash Reports: Technical information when the app crashes to help us fix bugs

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the 21days app functionality
  • Sync your habit data across devices (if you enable cloud sync)
  • Send you reminders and notifications (if enabled)
  • Improve our app and develop new features
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Ensure the security of our services

3. Data Storage and Security

Local-First Approach: By default, all your habit data is stored locally on your device. This means your data stays with you and is not uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly enable cloud sync.

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited access to personal data by our team

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data.

We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services that help us operate our app (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share your data

Third-Party Services We Use:

  • Firebase (Google) - Authentication and cloud storage
  • Expo - App development and updates

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and associated data (Learn how)
  • Export: Export your habit data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics and optional data collection

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at vijaynkw@gmail.com.

6. Children's Privacy

21days is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification for material changes
